Tax Slabs:
Slab1 = 0-100 = no tax
Slab2 = 100-200 = 10% tax
Slab3 = 200-400 = 20% tax
Slab4 = 400 above = 25% tax
Example 1: If income = 500
0-100 = 0
100-200 = 10% tax = 10
200-400 = 20% tax = 40
400+ = 25% tax = 25
Total Tax = 75
Example 2: If income = 350
0-100 = 0
100-200 = 10% tax = 10
200-400 = 20% tax = 30
400+ = 25% tax = 0
Total Tax = 40
